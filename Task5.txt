Syntax for Loops in Shell Scripting

1) While

while <condition>
do

    <command 1>
    <command 2>
    <etc>
done

2) For

for <var> in <value1 value2 ... valuen>
do
    <command 1>
    <command 2>
    <etc>
done

3) until

until <condition>
do

    <command 1>
    <command 2>
    <etc>
done
